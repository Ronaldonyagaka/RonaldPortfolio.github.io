SEASONAL FLU VACCINE ML PREDICTION

Overview

I developed a predictive model that forecasted individuals' decisions regarding the annual flu shot by analyzing historical data and identifying influential factors like age, gender, and household demographics. My primary objective was to accurately predict whether an individual would opt for the flu shot or not, ultimately assisting United States government health agencies in informed resource allocation and public health planning.

This portfolio project showcased my commitment to understanding the intricacies of flu shot uptake. I delved into demographic variables to uncover their statistical impact on vaccination choices, with a focus on targeting specific population segments for enhanced vaccination rates. My work culminated in the creation of a predictive model that determined an individual's likelihood to receive the seasonal flu vaccine based on key features and conditions, offering a valuable tool for public health professionals and contributing to healthier communities. BOOK RECOMMENDATION SYSTEM

Overview

In this project, our main objective was to develop a book recommendation system that provides personalized suggestions to users based on behaviors and preferences of similar users. We analyzed top book sales per city, identified the most popular books to infer genres, and determined the most popular authors. This data-driven approach allowed us to create a recommendation system that presents users with a curated list of book suggestions aligned with their tastes and interests.

Utilizing user interactions, historical data, and patterns, we harnessed insights gained from these sources to enhance the book discovery experience for users. We also assessed the voting frequency of users to further refine our recommendations. ANALYSIS OF SOLAR AND ELECT. GENERATION AND USAGE

Overview

This project focuses on utilizing the FB Prophet model to predict cost savings and assess the feasibility of integrating a battery system for storing surplus solar electricity. It involves analyzing historical data, forecasting cost savings, calculating price increases, evaluating Net Present Value (NPV), and analyzing Internal Rate of Return (IRR).

The analysis assumes consistent discount rates and minimal external influences on the dataset. By leveraging advanced predictive modeling and financial metrics, this project provides valuable insights into the potential financial benefits of adopting a solar battery system.
